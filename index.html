How I work

Small senior teams. Clear decisions. Shipping software.

Diagnose first

I get close to the code, the team and the decision-making before changing direction. Most delivery problems are not where people first point.

Stabilise, then accelerate

Fragile systems, unclear ownership and weak feedback loops slow everyone down. Fix those first, then speed compounds.

Keep humans where risk lives

AI can remove a lot of drag from delivery. It should not remove judgement from money, health data, security, compliance or trust.
